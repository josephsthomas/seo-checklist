How to Bake Sourdough Bread

Baking sourdough bread at home is a rewarding skill. This guide walks you through each step of the process.

What You Need

  • 500g bread flour
  • 100g active sourdough starter
  • 10g salt
  • 350ml water

Step 1: Feed Your Starter

Feed your sourdough starter 12 hours before baking. It should be bubbly and active.

Step 2: Mix the Dough

Combine flour, water, salt, and active starter. Mix until no dry flour remains.

Step 3: Bulk Fermentation

Let the dough rise for 4-6 hours. Perform stretch and folds every 30 minutes for the first 2 hours.

Step 4: Shape and Proof

Shape the dough and proof in the refrigerator overnight. This develops flavor and makes scoring easier.

Step 5: Bake

Bake in a Dutch oven at 450F for 45 minutes. Remove the lid for the last 15 minutes for a crispy crust.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does sourdough bread take to make?

From start to finish, sourdough bread takes about 24 hours. Most of that is passive waiting time.

Can I use all-purpose flour for sourdough?

Yes, you can use all-purpose flour. Bread flour will give you a better rise and chewier texture.
